Refunds FAQ

Q: How do I request a refund?

A: You can request a refund by contacting our customer support or through the 'Refunds' section in your account.

Q: How long will the refund process take?

A: Refunds typically take 3-5 business days to process, depending on your payment method.

Orders FAQ

Q: How can I track my order?

A: You can track your order through the 'My Orders' section in your account.

Q: Can I change my order after it's placed?

A: Orders can only be changed within 30 minutes of being placed. After that, you'll need to cancel and reorder.

Payments FAQ

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment options.

Q: Is it safe to enter my payment details?

A: Yes, we use SSL encryption to ensure that your payment details are always safe and secure.

Shipping FAQ

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: Shipping times vary by location, but generally, it takes 5-7 business days.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after placing the order?

A: Shipping addresses can only be updated before the order is dispatched. Please contact customer support for assistance.

Account Security FAQ

Q: How can I reset my password?

A: You can reset your password by clicking the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page.

Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication?

A: You can enable two-factor authentication in the 'Security' settings in your account.

Customer Support FAQ

Q: How can I contact customer support?

A: You can contact us via email, live chat, or the 'Contact Us' section in your account.

Q: What hours is customer support available?

A: Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any inquiries.
